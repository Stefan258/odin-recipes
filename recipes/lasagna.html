Recipe for delicious lasagna

Description

You have to try this DELICIOUS recipes asap

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • lasagna pasta
  • tomato sauce
  • parmegano cheese
  • cream cheese

Steps

  1. First, mix beef and tomato sauce together and simmer it for 30 minutes on medium flame.
  2. Then mix cream cheese and parmegano cheese together.
  3. Now, put 3 sheets of pasta on the bottom of the bowl and pour one third of beef tomato sauce on it and then pour your cheese sauce on it.
  4. Reapeat.
  5. Put in oven on 350 for 45 minutes.
